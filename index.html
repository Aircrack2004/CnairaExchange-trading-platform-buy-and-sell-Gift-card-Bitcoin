Currency Pairs We Trade

At Cnaira Exchange, we understand the importance of flexibility and variety in trading options. Here are some of the currency pairs that you can buy and sell on our platform:

  • Bitcoin (BTC) to USD
    Bitcoin (BTC) is the first decentralized digital currency, allowing peer-to-peer transactions without the need for intermediaries. It is exchanged for USD, providing a means for users to convert digital currency into traditional fiat money.
  • Ethereum (ETH) to USD
    Ethereum (ETH) is a blockchain platform known for its smart contract functionality. ETH, the platform's cryptocurrency, can be traded for USD. Ethereum enables developers to build decentralized applications (dApps) on its network.
  • Bitcoin (BTC) to EUR
    Bitcoin (BTC) to Euro (EUR) exchange rate allows users to convert Bitcoin into Euros. This pair is popular in Europe and among users who prefer trading in the Eurozone currency.
  • Ethereum (ETH) to EUR
    Ethereum (ETH) to Euro (EUR) exchange rate enables the trading of Ethereum for Euros. This is widely used by European traders and those dealing with the Euro currency.
  • Litecoin (LTC) to USD
    Litecoin (LTC) is a peer-to-peer cryptocurrency created as a lighter alternative to Bitcoin. It is often referred to as the silver to Bitcoin's gold. LTC can be traded for USD, offering a quick and low-cost means of transaction.
  • Ripple (XRP) to USD
    Ripple (XRP) is both a platform and a currency. The platform is an open-source protocol that is designed to allow fast and cheap transactions. XRP, its native cryptocurrency, can be exchanged for USD. Ripple aims to enable instant and secure global financial transactions.
  • Bitcoin Cash (BCH) to USD
    Bitcoin Cash (BCH) is a fork of Bitcoin created to address scalability issues. It allows for faster transaction times and lower fees. BCH can be traded for USD, providing an efficient alternative to Bitcoin for transactions.
  • Ethereum (ETH) to BTC
    Ethereum (ETH) to Bitcoin (BTC) exchange rate allows users to trade Ethereum for Bitcoin. This pair is often used by traders looking to diversify their cryptocurrency portfolios.
  • Litecoin (LTC) to BTC
    Litecoin (LTC) to Bitcoin (BTC) exchange rate enables the conversion of Litecoin into Bitcoin. This is useful for traders who wish to exchange their Litecoin holdings for Bitcoin.
  • Bitcoin Cash (BCH) to BTC
    Bitcoin Cash (BCH) to Bitcoin (BTC) exchange rate allows users to trade Bitcoin Cash for Bitcoin. This pair is popular among users who hold both cryptocurrencies and want to switch between them.
